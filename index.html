What is VanguardValues ?

VanguardValues is a test created by Albanian Bat Gaming#4569 aiming to classify political beliefs of people from the authoritarian left quadrant. This test assumes you are socialist and authoritarian.

What are the 16 values ?

There are 8 independent axes - Leader, Economic, Nation, Army, Class, Cultural, Social and Focus - and each has two opposing values assigned to them :

Demagogue ( Leader )
Those with higher Demagogue scores believe that a revolutionary is the best to lead. Those with high scores might support demagogy.

Technocrat ( Leader )
Those with higher Technocrat scores believe that we need competent leadership to rule the state. Those with high scores might support technocracy.

Planned ( Economic )
Those with higher Planned scores believe that the state should regulate the economy. Those with high scores might support planning.

Market ( Economic )
Those with higher Market scores believe that workers are able to decide production by themselves. Those with high scores might support laissez faire.

One Country ( Nation )
Those with higher One Country scores believe that socialism should be restricted to their country. Those with high scores might support chauvinism.

Global ( Nation )
Those with higher Global scores believe that all countries should adopt socialism. Those with high scores might support universalism.

Military ( Army )
Those with higher Military scores believe that weaponizing the proletariat is needed. Those with high scores might support juche.

Peace ( Army )
Those with higher Peace scores believe that socialism can be achieved peacefully. Those with high scores might support pacifism.

Proletarian ( Class )
Those with higher Proletarian scores believe that the focus of the revolution should be urban areas. Those with high scores might support industrialism.

Peasant ( Class )
Those with higher Peasant scores believe that rural farming regions are the priority of the revolution. Those with high scores might support agrarianism.

Uniform ( Cultural )
Those with higher Uniform scores believe that cultural conformity is the way to go. Those with high scores might support homogenity.

Tolerant ( Cultural )
Those with higher Tolerant scores believe that true socialism would accept all behaviors. Those with high scores might support diversity.

Traditional ( Social )
Those with higher Traditional scores believe that traditions are better for workers. Those with high scores might support reactionarism.

Neoteric ( Social )
Those with higher Neoteric scores believe that progress is linked with socialism. Those with high scores might support revolutionarism.

Populist ( Focus )
Those with higher Populist scores believe that the population should be taken in account. Those with high scores might support everyman.

Elitist ( Focus )
Those with higher Elitist scores believe that an elite should rule over the population. Those with high scores might support aristocracy.
